# Amazon Is Producing Garbage AI English Dubs

I’m incredibly disappointed to see Amazon produce AI English Dubs for Banana Fish and No Game No Life. These AI Dubs are soulless garbage that damages the creative works and ruins the anime viewing experience, in addition to being an absolute insult to the Voice Actors and customers. I’ve been waiting years for Banana Fish to get an English Dub but now I will never watch it, and I definitely won’t pay for it!
