# Entering That Flow State!

Sequence after sequence, a visual explosion of passion; experience the flash of inspiration, riding that incredible high of in the moment creating something, but also struggling with the void between those moments. Sadly, a personification of sacrificing one’s self for their craft in the churning system. Quirky, hilarious, charming, not your average isekai and my darkhorse favorite of the season!
