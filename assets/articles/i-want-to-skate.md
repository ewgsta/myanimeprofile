# I Want to Skate!

Adrenaline pumping, heart full of blazing passion, and I’ve found myself holding my breath, wincing, and getting a rush from every performance! The emotion and excitement is infectious. I love Inori and Tsukasa! Just pure love of the sport and drive to bring out the best inspiring one another. Tsukasa has become one of my favorite characters. No weird age gap romantic undertones. Crazy dynamic animation and banger OP and ED! Goodbye, thank you, and I am so hyped for season 2!
