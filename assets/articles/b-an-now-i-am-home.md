# bâan – Now I Am Home

It was over before I knew it. I didn’t expect such an immersive lived in world and a rollercoaster of emotions. The music by Kevin Penkin and English dub especially Sydney Poniewaz and Alex Le were perfect! Gigguk is my favorite creator. His works have made me bust out laughing and brought me to tears; and this was no different. He’s done it, he made an anime that I won’t forget for a long time!
