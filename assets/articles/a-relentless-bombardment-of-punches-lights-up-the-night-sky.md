# A Relentless Bombardment of Punches Lights Up the Night Sky

The mad lads of Ufotable went crazy hard and the soundtrack elevated every impact. What a rush, the hype is real! I needed a few days after watching for the dust to settle to inspect the emotional damage: this honestly put Demon Slayer up a notch for me. One of the best canonical anime movies I’ve seen and best seen on the big screen. I can’t wait for the next one!
