# A Gritty Sci-Fi Dystopian Criminal Underworld Chase! The 2000s are Back!

I get classic notes from the time of Ghost in the Shell and Cowboy Bebop. Slick fights, jazzy music, futuristic tech, and just the right amount of world collapse. Takes me back to Saturday nights watching anime and how fitting this is on Adult Swim. I have high hopes for this modern original from Shinichirou Watanabe.
